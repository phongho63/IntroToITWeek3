Ideal Project:

Overview

For my future project I will be making a chatterbot that interacts with the user and cheers them up when they’re sad or even be that friend that fully supports them when they have no one to support them ultimately improve or maybe even replace those negative feelings with better ones, the bot will not be an advisor but more of a motivator, filling them with a better perspective of the situation so they feel encouraged and let themselves make changes overall improving their life.

Motivation

Living in the 21st century I have noticed that the amount of people in across the world are suffering from depression, there are groups that grow stronger from fighting through depression, but there are also groups that unfortunately could not withstand the severe damages depression cause on them, some live with it, the other are not strong enough and choose the act of committing suicide. I myself had stepped down that path before but I was lucky that I had the support from my families. Knowing that everyone is not that lucky to have their family support them, I immediately asked myself what if there is an app that interacts with the users and is that supportive element. The best thing about it being an app is everyone can gain access to it globally, user-friendly because the program surrounds the chatting style system.

Description

The features will include a personal chatbox with the motivation chatterbox, where the user mainly interacts with, it will be like any other chatting app the user has experienced before, basic concept of sending and receiving, the choice of words are not limited as the bot will have a built-in function that detects words and keywords inside the user’s message, sending those keywords back to the database and from there choose the suitable branch of the situation and starts to ask questions back to the user to trim down the branch until the bot reaches the most suitable answer for the user, this does not only work for problems solving purposes but also work for normal everyday conversations, the bot will be able to have casual talks to the user if they chooses to approach the bot with that style. Besides that the app will have its own social media page that users can post anything they want and have other users reply anonymously, we tend to share our problems to the person that we have not met before more than the ones that are close to us, by being anonymous it will make the user feel more comfortable sharing. The app will also have a game that helps guide users to a healthier, better lifestyle, taking care of your virtual pets through using in-game currencies that can be obtained from finishing your projects, daily routines, picking up hobbies, and removing bad habits, whenever the user finishes something, they will tick that part off like a checklist and then gain the currencies. The purpose of giving the user the ability to give themselves credit is to not force the user to play the game but more of giving them a more fun and interactive system that helps them commit to change, the level of their pets and the health/stats will reflect back on the user’s journey. As of this moment that is all the features that I can think of, but I hope that as I progress through my course I will slowly get more ideas to innovate it.

Tools and Technologies

In order to create this project I would require a big amount of data storage to put in as many responses as possible for the bot to sort out the list. A suitable software that specialize in making chatting apps and software that makes the game that was mentioned on the previous topic. I will be making a list of questions consisting of multiple common problems that people encounter everyday to a large pool of anonymous responders to collect a good amount of answers to put into the bot’s database as well as small motivational quotes from famous people from the past to present that can go along with the bot message to somewhat assist the bot’s message.

Skills Required

The skills that are required for my project include experience in graphic designs to help make the overall app and the game more alive. Experience in common coding languages that can be used to make the app, psychology knowledge to create suitable question forms for the bot and implementing psychological analysis algorithms into the bot so it can detect what type of message and the core message from the user. I still do not know much about hardwares and what hardware-related things that I need to use in order to create the app, this will be further updated in the future. The chances of finding the skills, software and hardware that is required to make this project do-able is high since this will not be the first app that surrounds chatting with bot systems, there are a lot of apps that already let us talk to a bot so if I can apply the same method with my project and modify it so it works as what I intended it to, it will be done.

Outcome

If the project is successful, users will now have a partner that they can share their happy, sad moments with, a forum that shows the user that they are not the only ones that are struggling in life, by interacting with others they will form a stronger bond and ultimately make humans closer together, by playing the pet game the user will be motivated to follow a healthy schedule that will improve their life. The suicidal rate will decrease and the amount of people on the earth that suffers from depression will also appear less. Not to mention it is cheaper than going to see a psychiatrist, for those that do not have enough wealth to be able to seek help.
